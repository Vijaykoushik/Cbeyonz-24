Paper’nza


Description:

Interested participants opting for paper presentation should submit their domain of work and their abstract. A sample abstract will be attached for their reference. All the participants who registered in the google form will get a confirmation mail with an unique paper ID. The presenters with the ID will be called on the day of presentation for their chance of presenting. The calling order will be based on the team entry into the hall. Attendance will be maintained. The presenters will showcase their work through a presentation for 5 minutes followed by interactive Q & A. Once all the participants have completed their presentation, judges will evaluate and the prizes will be awarded during the validation ceremony


Topics:

Open domain. Examples are given below:

  • Artificial Intelligence ML
  • Internet of Things
  • Data Science
  • Programing
  • Cyber security
  • Networking
  • Blockchain

    • Team size: Max 3

    While submitting the google form, the team leader should enter his/her team members with contact details.
