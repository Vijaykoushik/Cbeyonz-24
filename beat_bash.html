BEAT BASH


Description:

A fun filled individual game featuring three levels centered around music and movies. Participants must complete tasks within the allocated time limit.


Rules & criteria:
  • Participants must adhere to the specified time limits for each level. Tasks should be completed within the allocated time.
  • Participants should not seek assistance from external sources, such as the internet or friends, during the game.
  • Participants are judged based on their cumulative completion time.
  • The winneris the one who finishes the game in the shortest overall time.
