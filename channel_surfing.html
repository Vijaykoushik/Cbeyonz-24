CHANNEL SURFING


Description:

Channel Surfing is about a team imitating the popular channels. The Judge will keep changing the channel and will give commands like (reverse, pause, play, fast-forward, slow motion) and the teams must perform accordingly. Cue will be given by judge to change the channel. A bell will be rung and a placard naming the category of channel will be shown. Performance must be changed immediately after that. According to the act points will be awarded. Categories of Channels: News, Sports, Movies-Hindi & English, Music-Hindi &English, Food Channels, Entertainment-National, Entertainment-International, Kids Channel, Lifestyle, Wildlife Channels, Science.


Rules & criteria:
  • Each team must have (Min. 2- Max. 3) members.
  • Performance timing is 4-6 minutes, no extra time will be given.
  • No props can be used No costume is to be used.
  • Do not use abusive words or actions in your Act.
  • Participants must refrain from opening their mouths or verbally stating the channel name during the game.
  • Participants are asked to be on time to allocated venue, else the team will be Disqualified.
  • Judge's decision is Final.


    • Team size: 2-3
